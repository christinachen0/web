Mediums

Acrylic paint was developed in the 1950's. The pigment is suspended in acrylic polymer emulsion has the characteristic of rubber. It is very fast drying, and will not decolor or crack over time.

Oil paint is considered the most versatile medium because you can thin it out or built up in more vibrant layers. One advantage of oil paint is that it does not dry as easily, which gives the artist time to blend or fix mistakes.

Styles

One of my favorite styles is abstract art. Abstract artists freely uses color, shapes, forms, and gestural art to represent an idea. It is up to the viewer to interpret what the art conveys. Famous abstract artists include Kandinsky, Pollock, and Rothko.
